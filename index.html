What Our Guests Say

I went to Savory Bite for a meal ; I left with a core memory.
The lobster bisque was pure comfort in a bowl. Rich, creamy, and perfectly seasoned, every spoonful delivered deep seafood flavor with a smooth, velvety texture. It felt luxurious from the first taste to the last drop.

The jellof rice was bursting with bold, smoky flavor and just the right blend of spices. Paired with the perfectly grilled chicken—juicy on the inside with a lightly crisp exterior—it was a truly satisfying and unforgettable meal.

The salmon was cooked to perfection—tender, flaky, and full of natural flavor. It melted in the mouth effortlessly, with a delicate seasoning that enhanced its freshness without overpowering it.

The chocolate lava cake was a dream for dessert lovers. Breaking into it released a warm, flowing chocolate center that was rich, smooth, and incredibly indulgent. Every bite felt like pure happiness.

The ice cream was silky, creamy, and delightfully refreshing. Its smooth texture and balanced sweetness made it the perfect treat to cool down and end the meal on a sweet note.

The espresso was bold and aromatic, with a deep, rich flavor that awakened the senses. Smooth yet powerful, it was the perfect finishing touch to a wonderful dining experience.

This summer special was bright and refreshing. The grilled lobster was tender and flavorful, while the citrus butter added a light, zesty twist that made every bite taste like sunshine on a plate.

Warm, creamy, and gently spiced, the pumpkin cream soup captured the true taste of autumn. It was comforting, smooth, and perfectly balanced with just the right hint of sweetness.

The roast duck was beautifully cooked, with crispy skin and juicy, tender meat. The orange glaze added a sweet and tangy flavor that elevated the dish into a winter masterpiece.

Fresh, vibrant, and beautifully balanced, the strawberry spinach salad combined crisp greens with sweet, juicy strawberries. It was light yet satisfying, with a refreshing flavor that made it simply delightful.
